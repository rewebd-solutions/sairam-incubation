Bookiva

LMES Coordinator: Jayandan S A (Chief)
Staff Coordinator: -
Project Start Date: 12-10-2021
Project End Date: 12-01-2022 (On-going)
Total Student count: 4

- Bookiva is a web application for reservations of college accommodations which works on any type of devices and will automatically detect and prevent users from booking rooms that have already been occupied for a cause. This preemptive method of detection and prevention eliminates the majority of office productivity related problems of having to find a vacant room. The major goal of this system is to totally digitise the records and eliminate the need for manual booking.

The user's room name and slot parameters are read from the user. The slot is compared to all other slots booked for that room on that exact day. If the session does not overlap with any other, it is available for booking. The booked slots details are stored in database and can be read from it
